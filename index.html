Overview

  • This is an android app for movie lovers.
  • Send movie suggestions to your friends.
  • Keep track of every movie you have suggested or have received.

How is it different?

  • No need to give a star or number rating to hundreds of movies because your friends send them directly to you.
  • Then you can put them in one of three categories: Must Watch, Recommended, or Worth Watching.

Moods

The app uses moods instead of genres because most of the time the genre doesn't help you pick a movie. When we are trying to pick something to watch we ask ourselves "What do I FEEL like to watching right now"?

The Categories:

Must Watch

Movies that excel in many of the aspects that make a great film. In other words, these are movies you just have to see before you die.

Recommended

Movies that are not necessarily award winning movies, but you recommend them because they are enjoyable or have something unique that sets them apart from the rest.

Worth Watching

Movies that even though you did not like the entire movie, they are still worth watching because they have at least one thing to offer. This could have been something you did not know, something to reflect on, or the movie was simply just different from anything you had ever seen before.
